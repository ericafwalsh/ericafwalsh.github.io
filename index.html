About Me

Hello everyone! Welcome to my first website. I'd love to tell you a little about myself, and my current "side hustle" - learning to code!

I have a rather extensive education in biology, and bats specifically (more on that later). Coming out of that experience, I had my first hard pivot in my professional career - joining a fast moving ecommerce startup. Here I was immersed in the tech industry and had a front row seat to tech innovation within retail. Working as a buyer in the consumable space, I have pretty much become an expert in soap. Welcome to my next pivot - coding class! I am now dedicating my Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays, and let's be honest, every other free moment, to learning full stack web development.

Low and behold, my first official (live) website in my early web development career. I hope you enjoy it, and I welcome any feedback you have. Thank you!
